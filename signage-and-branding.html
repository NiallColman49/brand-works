FLAT PANEL SIGNS

Budget-friendly and fast turnaround make flat panel signage a popular choice for startups, getting their brand noticed without breaking the bank.

Ditch the bulky signs and embrace a modern look with flat panel signage! This technology utilizes thin, lightweight panels that showcase your brand with vibrant colors and high-resolution images, stopping passersby in their tracks. Flat panels are energy-efficient compared to traditional illuminated signs, saving you money in the long run.

They're also lightweight, making installation a breeze and minimizing disruption to your business. The real win for startups? Flat panel signage is the most cost-effective option, perfect for those tight budgets. Plus, the quick turnaround time allows you to get your brand noticed fast.

TRAY SIGNS

Make a bold statement with sign trays. Crafted from folded aluminum Dibond, these signs project a sleek, 3D profile.

Craving a signage solution that truly stands out? Look no further than sign trays. These innovative signs are crafted from high-quality, folded aluminum Dibond, ensuring a sleek and durable build.

Unlike traditional flat signs, sign trays boast a unique 3D profile. Here's the magic: all four edges of the sign are meticulously folded and joined, creating a hidden compartment within the "tray" itself. This clever design allows for all mounting hardware to be concealed, leaving a clean, modern aesthetic that complements any building fascia or entrance. The result? A sign that projects a sense of quality and professionalism, guaranteed to grab attention and make a lasting impression.

CUT LETTERS

Dimensional flat cut letters & logos in various materials, finishes & sizes. Expert installation & customization available.

Tired of generic signage? Flat-cut lettering offers endless possibilities! Brand Works craft custom signs to perfectly promote your brand. We use precise laser cutting to create any letter, pattern, or logo, in a variety of materials like foam PVC, acrylic, and aluminum composite. Choose from mirrored, brushed, or plain finishes, and even add vinyl for extra impact.

Beyond customisation, flat-cut lettering boasts several advantages. They're lightweight for and can be tailored to both indoor and outdoor use. The durable materials we use ensure your sign withstands the elements and maintains its vibrant appearance for years to come. Plus, flat-cut lettering offers a clean, professional aesthetic that complements any business.

PAVEMENT SIGNS

Pavement signs, those trusty sidewalk standouts, are more than just eye-catchers. They're a silent salesperson, working tirelessly to grab attention and get customers through your door. Here's why pavement signs are a must-have for any business:

Boost foot traffic:
Bold colors, clear messaging, and strategic placement make pavement signs impossible to miss, enticing passersby to explore what you offer.

Promote irresistible offers:
Highlight limited-time discounts, new product launches, or special events. Pavement signs are perfect for showcasing deals that will get customers excited to step inside.

Be flexible and creative:
Update your message regularly to keep things fresh and promote seasonal offerings. Pavement signs are perfect for A/B testing different messages to see what resonates most with your audience.

Cost-effective advertising:
Compared to other advertising options, pavement signs are a budget-friendly way to reach a wide audience. They're a high-impact, low-cost investment in your business growth.
