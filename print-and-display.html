MEASURABLE SALES

For promotional displays specifically tied to a particular product or service, you can track sales figures before, during, and after the campaign to see if there's a measurable uplift.

INCREASED BRAND AWARENESS

Eye-catching displays put your brand in front of potential customers, increasing brand awareness and recall. This can lead to increased website traffic, phone calls, or foot traffic to your store. You can track these metrics to understand the reach of your campaign.

TARGETED REACH

You can strategically place print and display materials to reach specific demographics or locations. Counting foot traffic after a display is installed in a high-visibility area can give you a sense of its effectiveness.

IMPROVED BRAND CREDIBILITY

High-quality printed materials project a professional image and build trust with potential customers. While it's difficult to directly measure the impact on sales, improved brand perception can translate to higher customer lifetime value.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

If you have any questions about our services, experience or projects, find answers to some common client questions below, or get in touch with our team who will be happy to help.

Do I need to clean my vehicles before wrapping?

To ensure a smooth installation and get your branded vehicle back to you fast, we kindly ask that you pre - wash your vehicle before bringing it in. Here's what we recommend:

• Simple Soap & Water: A thorough wash with mild soap and water is all you need. • Skip the Polish: Avoid using waxes or polishes, as these can affect the adhesion of the vinyl.
Heavily soiled vehicles may require additional cleaning prior to installation, which can impact your scheduled completion time. An additional cleaning fee of £50+VAT may apply in these cases.

How long does a vehicle wrap last?

Professionally installed vehicle wraps can become powerful brand ambassadors for years to come. At Brand Works, we're passionate about creating wraps that endure the elements and turn heads. We use only the highest - quality materials and employ expert installation techniques to ensure your wrap looks amazing and delivers lasting value. In fact, we've seen some incredibly well - maintai ned wraps last up to 8 years! Proper care and avoiding extreme weather conditions will significantly extend the lifespan of your wrap. But with our commitment to quality and craftsmanship, you can be confident your investment will deliver long - lasting results.

I don't have any artwork, can you create it for me?

Of course we can. At Brand Works, our in - house designers have worked in the industry over 15 years, so our team are used to working closely with our customers to help bring their vision to life , just provide a design brief, any colours/text, images you would like and we will do the rest.
